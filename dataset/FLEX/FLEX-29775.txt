Summary:
When a combo box drop down is in the form of a DataGrid QTP does not record the events performed on the Datagrid.
Description:
Steps to reproduce:
1.Open a test application that contains a combo box with a drop down in the form of a datagrid.
2.Click on Automation->Record.
3.Click on a line in the datagrid drop down or resize the datagrid columns
4.Stop recording.
Actual Results: None of the Datagrid events are being recorded by QTP.
Expected Results:The events performed on the datagrid should be recorded into the QTP script.
