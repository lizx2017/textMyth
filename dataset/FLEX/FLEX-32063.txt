Summary:
AdvancedDataGrid column sort arrow icon not updating correctly
Description:
Steps to reproduce:
1. Take the general AdvancedDataGrid sample app.
2. Goto the header of column 1 and click at the number with the arrow icon.
3. Click at the number with the arrow icon again and notice that the arrow icon does not update until you move your mouse off the column header.
Actual Results:
The arrow icon does not change until you move your mouse off the column header.
Expected Results:
The arrow icon should update instantly.
Workaround (if any):
See this duplicate issue for a workaround / fix: http://bugs.adobe.com/jira/browse/FLEXDMV-1382
