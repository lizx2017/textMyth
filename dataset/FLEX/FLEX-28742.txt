Summary:
[iOS] Pressing on the border of a TextInput closes the SK and leaves a focus ring on the TextInput
Description:
Steps to reproduce:
1. Press in the first TextInput, opening the softkeyboard
2. Press on the bottom border of the second TextInput
Actual Results:
SoftKeyboard closes. Focus ring is drawn around the 2nd TextInput. The second TextInput's StageText doesn't have focus.
Expected Results:
The SK remains open. Focus ring is drawn around the 2nd TextInput. Focus is in 2nd TextInput's StageText

none
