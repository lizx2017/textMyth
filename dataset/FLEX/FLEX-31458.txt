Summary:
ADG corrupts the selection border for all components in design view
Description:
Steps to reproduce:
1. Place an ADG and a Button control in Design view.
2. Click on ADG and verify that selection border appears.
3. Move the ADG around the stage and leave the mouse.
4. Select ADG or Button component by clicking on it.
Actual Results:
No selection border is seen.
Expected Results:
Selection border should be seen for all components.
Workaround (if any):
Click on "Refresh" icon to see the selection border again.
