Summary:
Mobile: Textinput in Actionbar
Description:
For Flasbuilder 4.6 BETA
Steps to reproduce:
1. Create a mobile ViewNavigatorApplication Project with 2 or more views
2. insert a textinput in the actionbar on view2
3. Push into the view 2 and enter a text in the textinput
4. When you poptoView into the view1, the text stand already in the actionbar on view1 !
Actual Results:
The text fromt the textinput from view2 stay in the actionbar from view1
Expected Results:
No text in view 1
Workaround (if any):
No workaround. Or delete textinput field on exit Event handler from view2 ?!
