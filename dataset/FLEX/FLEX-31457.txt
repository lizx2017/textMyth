Summary:
Data inside ADG disappears when ADG is moved or resized in Design view
Description:
Steps to reproduce:
1. Place an ADG of "tree" or "flat" type in design view.
2. Click on ADG and move the component around.
Actual Results:
The Tree or Flat data displayed inside the ADG disappears.
Expected Results:
The data should remain same even after moving or resizing.
Workaround (if any):
Click on "Refresh" icon displays the ADG data properly.
