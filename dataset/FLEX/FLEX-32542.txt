Summary:
Clicking outside an AdvancedDataGrid causes change of selectedItem
Description:
Steps to reproduce:
1. Click on column Name on Material 1005 to open itemEditor.
2. Double-click some other row in the datagrid, for example material 1003.
3. Data for selected material is loaded.
4. Click somewhere on the screen (outside the data grid).
Actual Results:
Data for selected material gets replaced with the data of material 1005 (previously selected for editing).
Expected Results:
Selected material should not change.
