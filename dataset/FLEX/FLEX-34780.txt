Content of status bar not shown in landscape mode
Description:
The content of the status bar (clock, carrier-, wifi-, battery indicator) is not shown in landscape mode after upgrading to Apache Flex 4.14.0. Only the background of the status bar is shown.
Test cases:
In portrait mode, the status bar is correctly rendered; only landscape mode is affected
In Apache Flex 4.13, the status bar is correctly rendered
The use of LegacyMobileThemeOverride option has no effect
