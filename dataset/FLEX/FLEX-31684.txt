Summary:
Changing any field in 'Sort Display' category of ADG in CSS Design view does not refresh the preview
Description:
Steps to reproduce:
1. Add a style for AdvancedDataGrid in CSS Design view.
2. Switch to Design tab and change the "Font size" field under "Sort Display" category to 14
Actual Results:
Preview is not refreshed with the change in current Font size.
Expected Results:
Preview is SHOULD be refreshed whenever a field is changed in "Sort Display" category.
Workaround (if any):
Click on "Refresh" icon or select "Design->Refresh" menu item.
