Summary:
Problem with scale (scaleX and scaleY) of AdvancedDataGrid and Legend
Description:
Steps to reproduce:
1. Run attached swf
2. Move the VSlider to 0.97 and click the 'Create PieChart Legend'
3. Mouse over the header of the AdvancedDataGrid
Actual Results:
a - The legend is horizontally. This occurs if the scale is changed before creating the legend.
b - The label of the header is cut. This occurs when using certain scales (0.97 for example).
Expected Results:
a - The legend is vertical.
b - The label can not be cut.
Workaround (if any):
a - Change the scale of the legend before create it. Just change the VSlider before click in the button.
