Summary:
PrintOLAPDataGrid does not display horizontal scrollbar after the design view is refreshed
Description:
Steps to reproduce:
1. Place PrintOLAPDataGrid in the source view of application
2. Set "horizontalScrollPolicy" of PODG to "on"
3. Switch to Design view and confirm that horizontal scrollbar of PODG is displayed
4. Click on "Refresh" icon or "Design->Refresh" menu in Design view
Actual Results:
Horizontal scrollbar of PODG is not seen
Expected Results:
Horizontal scrollbar of PODG is should be seen as soon as the design view is refreshed
Workaround (if any):
Move the PODG on the stage to see the horizontal scrollbar
