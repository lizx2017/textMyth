1. Start recording on dashboard sample
2. click on item on line chart on left side pane
3. click near the item (click when datatip appears)
Actual Results:
click is recorded sometimes (when mouse pointer is not placed precisely) as chart click
Expected Results:
click should get recorded as series/item click when item click action is there and chart click otherwise
