Summary:
Using Embeddedfonts along with fontSize corrupts ADG headers while scrolling
Description:
Steps to reproduce:
1. Compile & Launch the attached application
2. Scroll down the ADG
3. Scroll down the DG

ADG's header corrupts while scrolling. DG's header does not corrupt even after scrolling.
Expected Results:
ADG's header should not corrupt even while using Embedded fonts with fontSize specified.
Workaround (if any):
