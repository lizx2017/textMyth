Summary:
[AIr Specfic] Could not connect to QTP alert message is not formatted correctly
Description:
Steps to reproduce:
1.Open QTP
2.Run an air app enabled with automation and see that it has established a connection with QTP.
3.Now forcefully terminate the AIr app and try running it again.
Actual Results:you will get an alert box with "Could not connect to QTP. Either QTP is not started or may be more than one automation enabled application running. Please close all instances, restart QTP, restart application." message .
Expected Results:The message should be "Could not connect to QTP. Either QTP is not started or there may be more than one automation enabled application running. Please close all instances of the application, restart QTP and then restart the application."
