Summary:
ADG border disappears on state transition
Description:
(We're using Flex Builder with Flex 3.2.0, also verified on Flex 3.4)
Removing and then adding an AdvancedDataGrid using states causes border of the AdvancedDataGrid to disappear.
To reproduce, this app and click on the 'hide' button, then click on the button. The ADG's border will be gone. Sorting or resizing a column will cause the border to draw again.
