Description:

http://stackoverflow.com/questions/33044069/pdfbox-2-0-and-ttc-fonts

        TrueTypeFont ttFont = null;
        for (TrueTypeFont font : trueTypeCollection.getFonts())

            if ("MS-Gothic".equals(font.getName()))
